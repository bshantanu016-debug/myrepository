# AI-powered SEO content production

## Topic
AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Objective
To analyze how leading SEO experts are adapting their strategies in the era of AI-driven content creation, with a focus on content quality, scalability, and search intent alignment.

## Why This Topic?
AI has significantly changed how content is created and scaled. While it enables faster production, it also increases competition and risks around content quality. This research focuses on how experienced SEO professionals are approaching this shift and maintaining effective strategies.

## Expert Selection Criteria
The selected experts are active SEO practitioners who consistently share practical insights on content strategy, technical SEO, and evolving search trends. Their work reflects real-world adaptation to AI-powered content production.

## Data Sources
This research is primarily based on LinkedIn posts to capture recent, real-time insights directly from industry experts.

## Data Collection Method
- Collected recent LinkedIn posts manually from selected experts  
- Extracted key ideas and patterns from their content  

### Future Scope (API-Based Expansion)
To scale this research further:
- YouTube transcripts can be collected using APIs  
- Data extraction can be automated using scraping tools  
- Content can be structured programmatically for larger datasets  

## Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md` → List of selected experts with references  
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` → Posts organized by each expert  
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` → Planned for future API-based data collection  
- `/research/other/` → Additional notes and observations  

## Key Focus Areas
- AI-assisted content creation  
- Content quality vs scale  
- Search intent optimization  
- Technical SEO supporting content  
- Changing search behavior in the AI era  

## Workflow
- Identified relevant SEO experts  
- Collected their recent LinkedIn posts  
- Extracted key insights  
- Organized content into structured folders  
- Maintained incremental commits to reflect workflow  

## Conclusion
The research shows that while AI is transforming content production, effective SEO still depends on strong fundamentals such as user intent, content quality, and technical optimization. AI enhances efficiency, but strategic execution remains the key to success.
